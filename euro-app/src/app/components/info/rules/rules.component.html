Guesses

  • Place your guesses in the edit Guesses Page
  • You may change them only before the match begins.
  • You only need to guess who wins, (or a tie).
  • In knockout games, guess the result up to 120 minutes, not including penalties, so a tie is possible.
  • You can't see what other players guessed before the match begins.
  • The Home Page home shows you what your friends have guessed and how they have scored, in all past matches.

Scoring

Each match has a pre-allocated amount of points. The points will be distributed evenly between players who have guessed correctly. So the more players guess the match result correctly, the less points each player receives.

Example: if the match is worth 120 points, and 4 players have guessed it correctly, they each receive 30 points.

Strategy

  • The less players guess the match correctly, the more points each one gets. Therefore, it is a good idea to bet on supprises every once in a while.
  • Scoring a Solo Bet new_releases, where you are the only player in your group to have guessed the score, earns you a substantial amount of points.
  • However, it is not a good idea to look only for surprises. The winners are usually those who find the right balance between surprises and predicted results (and of course those who guess correctly insert_emoticon)
