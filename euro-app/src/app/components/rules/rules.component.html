How to play

Guesses

You may place your guesses in the Guesses Page edit. You may change your guess at any time as long as the match has not started. You only need to guess the result (who wins, or if there was a tie). For knockout games, you guess the result after 90 or 120 minutes, not including penalties, so a tie is possible.

You will not be able to see what other players in your group have guessed before the match starts. You can, however, see what your friends have guessed, and how they have scored, in all matches that have already started. To see this, go to the Home Page home

Scoring

Each match has a pre-allocated amount of points. The points will be distributed evenly between players who have guessed the result of the match correctly. So the more players guess the match result correctly, the less points each player receives.

Example: if the match is worth 20 points, and 4 players have guessed it correctly, they each receive 5 points.

Strategy

Since you get more points for guessing the results correctly, if as few as possible players have also guessed it correctly, it is a good idea to bet on supprises every once in a while. Scoring a Solo Bet new_releases, where you are the only player in your group, who have guessed the score correctly, can earn you a substantial amount of points. However, it is not a good idea to look only for surprises. The winners are usually those who find the right balance between surprises and predicted results (and of course those who guess correctly insert_emoticon)
